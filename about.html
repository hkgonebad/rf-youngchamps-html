The Reliance Foundation Young Champs academy (RFYC) kicked off in May 2015 with the vision of creating not only the nation’s next generation of football stars, but also its future role models and leaders.

Supported entirely by the Reliance Foundation, RFYC is a one of a kind full time residential football academy offering all expenses paid scholarships to young talented footballers from across the country.

RFYC Academy Timeline

2015

Academy opens its doors

The Academy welcomed its first intake of scholars for its u-12, u-13, and u-14 teams.

2017

Voted India’s Best Football Academy

The Academy is voted as India’s Best Football Academy, earning 4 star rating from the AIFF.

2017

Young Champs head to RCP

The Reliance Foundation builds a World Class training facility for the Young Champs at Reliance Corporate Park.

2019

Building on Previous Success

The Academy earns a highest possible 5/5 star rating from the AIFF (becoming India’s first ever 5 star rated Academy)

2019

RFYC joins the Youth League

RFYC joins the Hero India Youth League, India’s foremost elite - level league for youth teams.

2020

AFC recognizes RFYC

The Asian Football Confederation (AFC).confers a 2 star rating upon RFYC. The highest rating given to an Indian Academy!

2020

First batch of players graduates

The first batch of RFYC scholars graduates from the Academy! 9 players sign professional contracts with Indian clubs.
